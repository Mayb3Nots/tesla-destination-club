# Tesla Destination Club

Fair, stress-free destination charging — for every Tesla owner.

## The Problem

Tesla destination chargers in Malaysia are free — but "free for all" doesn't mean fair for all.
Without coordination, you get overstayers, surprise queues, and no way to know if a charger
will be free when you arrive. The existing solution is a WhatsApp group. It works, but it doesn't scale.

## What We're Building

A lightweight coordination layer on top of the community that already exists. Tesla Destination Club
lets you book a slot, track the live queue, and get notified when it's your turn

**App users get a better experience. Non-app users aren't left behind.**

We're starting with a web app to keep things simple and accessible — no install required.

## Our Philosophy on Physical Queuers

The app exists to serve the community — not to give app users an unfair advantage.

If someone is physically present and waiting at the charger, they take priority. Always.
No exceptions, no debate. If that means giving up your booked slot, that's the right call.

The queue is a coordination tool, not a reservation system. Real people in real life come first.

Not everyone is on this platform yet — and that's okay. Be patient, be gracious,
and remember that the goal is a better experience for the whole community, not just app users.

## Core Features

- **Slot booking** — reserve a time at a destination charger so everyone gets a fair turn
- **Live queue** — see who's currently charging, how long they have left, and your position in line
- **Community reports** — flag when a charger is occupied by a non-app user so the queue stays accurate
- **WhatsApp sharing** — share your booking details to your community group in one tap,
  so non-app users are always in the loop

## Coming Soon
